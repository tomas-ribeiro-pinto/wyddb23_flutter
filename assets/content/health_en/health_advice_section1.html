WYD DON BOSCO 23 Health Team

*break*

The WYD DON BOSCO 23 Health Team was created with the aim of providing primary health care to pilgrims registered for World Youth Day Lisbon 2023 by the Salesian Youth Movement, as well as to the organisation's volunteers and support teams in the accommodation sites.

The team is made up of more than 40 volunteers and includes, in addition to doctors and nurses, firefighters and a cardiopneumology technician.
